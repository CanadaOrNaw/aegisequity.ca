Who It's For

Good Fit

You have a specific physical problem — a custom-fit part, a device that needs to run off-grid, an enclosure that doesn't exist, a mount for something unusual — and you can't find an off-the-shelf answer.

  • You need a custom bracket, jig, or fixture that isn't worth a full manufacturing run
  • You have a device that needs solar or battery power — no plug nearby
  • You need a custom enclosure, mount, or adapter measured to exact specs
  • You want a self-sufficient setup: Meshtastic repeater, sensor station, remote monitoring
  • You have the idea but not the design and fabrication tools to execute it

Not a Fit

  • You need a part you can already buy online
  • You need licensed engineering sign-off, electrical certification, or structural certification
  • You need structural load-bearing or life-safety work
  • You expect guaranteed fit before any prototype cycle — custom work iterates
  • You need mass production (we do one-offs and small runs)

How It Works

Measure. Design. Prototype. Iterate. Deliver.

1

Measure

Calipers, photos, constraints. On-site measurement or you provide the specs. We document exactly what the problem needs.

2

Design

First draft: 3D model, wiring plan, or both. You see the design before anything is built. Materials list prepared and quoted.

3

Prototype

Physical V1 — printed, assembled, tested against the real problem. This is where custom work proves itself.

4

Iterate & Deliver

Refine until it fits. Two iterations included in every quote — extras billed transparently. You get the finished part, built and tested.

What We Don't Do

Aegis designs and builds custom physical solutions. We do not provide engineering code-compliance sign-off, electrical certification, or structural certification. We don't take on safety-critical work (structural load-bearing, life-safety systems) without a separate risk conversation. We don't guarantee fit before any prototype cycle — custom physical work iterates. We're not a mass production shop.
