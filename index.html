systems

Hardware, power, software tools, and the glue between them.

The offer is not “app development” and it is not just 3D printing. It is bounded custom technology: specific problem, clear deliverable, tested handoff.

physical

Custom parts and enclosures

Brackets, mounts, adapters, jigs, fixtures, housings, and one-off mechanical pieces measured to fit the real object.

  • 3D modelling
  • prototype cycles
  • small runs
power

Solar and battery setups

Device-level solar, trickle charging, backup power, panel placement, battery sizing, and wiring-route planning.

embedded

IoT and local devices

Meshtastic nodes, sensors, repeaters, microcontrollers, local monitoring, and weather-ready enclosures.

software

Dashboards, interfaces, and small tools

Camera dashboards, monitoring panels, local device interfaces, data utilities, API connections, and bounded automations. No giant SaaS promises.

creative

Maker-style builds

Hardware, software, power, fabrication, and installation planning combined into one useful finished thing.

process

Every project starts as a scope, not a guess.

01listen

What needs to exist?

02map

Measure the space, device, power, and data constraints.

03build

Prototype the smallest useful version.

04handoff

Deliver the thing, files, notes, and support path.

Aegis does not provide licensed engineering sign-off, electrical certification, structural certification, life-safety systems, medical devices, weapons, or mass production. Licensed electrical work is handled by a licensed electrician; Aegis can design and plan around it.
