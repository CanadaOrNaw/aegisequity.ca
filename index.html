What We Don't Do

We design, build, and install custom technology. We do not provide licensed engineering sign-off, electrical certification, or structural certification. We don't do licensed electrical work (mains, panel, permanent wiring) — we design and plan it; a licensed electrician executes. We don't take on structural load-bearing or life-safety systems without a separate risk conversation. We don't guarantee results before any prototype cycle — custom work iterates. We're not a mass production shop.
